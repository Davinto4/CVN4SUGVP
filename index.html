Dear EBSU students,

My name is Chukwu Victoria Nnenna, a proud student of this great institution, and with humility and a heart to serve, I’m officially declaring my interest to run for the office of the SUG Vice President.

As we prepare for this journey, I ask for your support, your voice, and your trust. Together, we can build a stronger, more inclusive student community.
